About Me

I was born and raised in Ipswich, Australia. I grew up in a sporting household playing basketball, tennis and cricket. After finishing high school I eventually joined the Queensland Police and performed first reponse duties in South Brisbane and North Queensland.

In 2010 I got married and from December 2010 to December 2011 as a honeymoon, my wife and I backpacked around the world. We returned to North Queensland and our previous occupations and almost immediately I obtained a job as an investigator in the Criminal Investigation Branch, South Brisbane. From 2012 on I worked towards making Detective which I achieved in January 2016 and soon found myself partnered with junior investigators and coordinating major investigations.

In this time, my wife had our 3 children and it is for them, that I quit my job now find myself travelling the world.

What matters to me now:

  • My family
  • Living Life
  • Seeing it All

One more thing...
I have a desire to learn, to work and to help others which is why I've chosen this course.
